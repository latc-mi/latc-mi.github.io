Meet Day Checklist

Be prepared and ready to compete at your best.

🎒 What to Bring

  • LATC uniform (top + shorts/spandex)
  • Running shoes (and spikes if you have them)
  • Water bottle — stay hydrated!
  • Healthy snacks for between events
  • Sunscreen and hat for outdoor meets
  • Layers/warm-up clothes for cool mornings
  • Your event schedule printed or on your phone
  • Positive attitude and team spirit! 💪

⏰ Meet Day Timeline

  • 🕗
    Arrive Early Plan to arrive 45–60 minutes before your first event
  • 🏃
    Warm-Up Team warm-up with coaches 30 minutes before events
  • 📢
    Check In Report to the clerk of course when your event is called
  • 🏅
    Compete & Cheer Support your teammates when you're not competing!

51st Annual Championship Meet

July 22, 2026 — the Grand Ledge Summer Track & Field Championship. All events are timed finals and eligible for records.

📍 Meet Details

  • 🗓️
    Date Wednesday, July 22, 2026
  • 🏆
    Format Championship Meet — all events are timed finals and eligible for records. Medals to the top 3 places in individual events and to members of the 1st-place relay teams.
  • 📋
    Entries Maximum of 4 events per athlete, including relays
  • 🎂
    Age groups Age as of meet day. Male and female events are run separately, except the 3200m.
  • 🏟️
    Location Grand Ledge Track Complex
  • 🗺️
    Address 1211 Lohne Dr., Grand Ledge, MI 48837

🎟️ Registration

  • 💵
    Drop-in fee $15 at the gate for the Championship Meet
  • ✅
    Already pre-registered? The $80 season registration includes the Championship Meet — nothing more to pay
  • 🌐
    Pre-register online www.grandledgecomets.org
  • 📞
    Questions? Contact Rich White at 517-214-2958

Discus & shot put start at 6:00 PM, all other field events at 6:30 PM, and running events at 7:30 PM.

Notes

  • At ribbon meets, every individual event awards ribbons for 1st – 5th place.
  • At the championship meet, all events are timed finals, eligible for records, with medals to the top finishers.
  • The 4 & under age group may compete in Long Jump, Softball Throw, and running distances of 400m or less at every meet.
  • Hurdles are run at MHSAA heights. Line-ups are based on last year's cycle — check the meet pages and Facebook for weekly updates.

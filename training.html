Training Tips

Advice from the coaches to help every athlete perform their best.

💧 Stay Hydrated

Drink water throughout the day — not just during practice. Bring a water bottle to every session and aim for at least 8 glasses per day. On hot days, increase your intake.

😴 Prioritize Sleep

Athletes need 8–10 hours of sleep per night. Sleep is when your body actually improves — muscle repair, memory consolidation, and growth happen during rest.

🥗 Fuel Right

Eat a balanced meal 2–3 hours before practice. Include carbohydrates for energy, protein for muscle repair, and plenty of fruits and vegetables for vitamins.

🧘 Warm Up & Cool Down

Never skip your warm-up or cool-down. Dynamic warm-ups prepare your muscles and reduce injury risk. Cool-down stretching prevents soreness and improves flexibility.

🧠 Mental Preparation

Visualize your race before meets. Practice positive self-talk and focus on what you can control. A strong mental approach is just as important as physical training.

🩹 Listen to Your Body

There's a difference between normal workout soreness and pain from injury. If something hurts, tell a coach immediately. It's always better to rest a day than to miss weeks.

About these records

  • Compiled from the 1973–2018 master records PDF plus championship updates from 2022 (47th annual) and 2023 (48th annual). Records set in 2022–2023 are highlighted.
  • Not yet included: 2019 (46th), and 2024 (49th, "33 records broken") and 2025 (50th) — those results were only shared as photos / an emailed PDF.
  • A few ⚑ marks are flagged where a meet sheet highlighted a record that did not surpass the documented standard — kept the documented mark pending reconciliation.
  • Questions or corrections? Contact Grand Ledge Summer Track on Facebook.
